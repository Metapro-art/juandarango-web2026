About

Based in Bogotá, Juan D Arango is a guitarist, composer and producer who treats improvisation as the architecture of his music. He performs with AKA Trio and Mèdulla, produces interdisciplinary work under the name AHUM, and teaches guitar at Universidad Sergio Arboleda and Universidad Pedagógica Nacional.
